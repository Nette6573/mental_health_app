The Mental Health Challenge in Jamaica

Many individuals face significant barriers to accessing mental health support

Limited Awareness

Many Jamaicans lack awareness of mental health conditions and available interventions.

Stigma & Shame

Cultural stigma prevents many from seeking the psychological help they need.

Access Barriers

Limited access to licensed professionals, especially outside urban centers.

Spiritual Disconnect

Lack of integration between spiritual support and mental health resources.
